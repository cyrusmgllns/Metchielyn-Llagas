Passion

One of my passion is diving into the world of paintings isn't just a hobby for me; it's a strong fascination that adds color and deep thoughts to my life. What captivates me about paintings is how they go beyond the canvas, capturing human feelings, creativity, and history. Whether it's the classics from famous artists or the expressive styles of today, each painting feels like a doorway to a different world, creating a beautiful visual experience that connects with my emotions. Whether I'm finding comfort in old favorites or exploring new, modern art, my love for paintings is a continual journey. Every brushstroke and color add another layer to my understanding and admiration for the amazing beauty that artists bring to life on the canvas.

I also like photography. I enjoy the view of the nature so I always captured it. Photography is more than just a hobby for me; it's a profound passion that frames my perspective on the world. Through the lens, I find a powerful means of expression, capturing moments that might otherwise slip away. It's not just about taking pictures; it's about freezing time, creating a visual narrative, and finding beauty in the ordinary. Photography, for me, is a journey of discovery, where every click of the shutter opens a new chapter in my ongoing exploration of the visual world.
